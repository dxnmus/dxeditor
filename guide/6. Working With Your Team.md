# Working With Your Team

## Workspaces

A workspace is just a folder on your computer. You can connect as many as you
like: your personal drafts plus each shared folder. Switch between them from
the dropdown at the top of the sidebar, and use **Add workspace** to connect a
new one.

## How team sync works

Point your workspace at your OneDrive-synced copy of a shared folder. Everyone
edits normally, and OneDrive keeps the files in sync. Because syncing happens
through OneDrive rather than instantly, this suits "we each work on different
notes."

## The safety net

If a teammate's edit reaches your disk while you were writing the same note,
you get a banner with **Take theirs** or **Keep mine** instead of a silent
overwrite. The second save never quietly erases the first.

## Comments for review

- Select text in a note and leave a comment.
- Tag it **🤖 For agent** or **👥 For team**.
- Commented text is highlighted. A side panel shows threads with reply and
  resolve.
- Comments live in a sidecar file next to the note, so the note itself stays
  clean.

"For team" comments are notes to your colleagues. "For agent" comments are
instructions for a Claude Code pipeline, covered in
[The Agent Workflow](<8. The Agent Workflow.md>).

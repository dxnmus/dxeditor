# Welcome to DXEditor

DXEditor is a clean writing app for a folder of plain markdown files on your
computer. You point it at a folder and get a Notion-style editor, while your
notes stay as portable `.md` files on disk. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

## Do this first

1. This "DXEditor Guide" workspace is your manual. Read through it using the
   sidebar on the left.
2. When you are ready to write, switch to your own workspace using the
   dropdown at the top of the sidebar.
3. To connect a real folder, like a shared OneDrive folder, open that dropdown
   and choose **Add workspace**.

# Updating the App

DXEditor keeps itself up to date. You do not need to reinstall it by hand.

## How it works

The app checks for a new version when it starts, and every few hours while it
stays open. You can also check any time from the menu: **DXEditor > Check for
Updates** on Mac, or **Help > Check for Updates** on Windows.

## On Windows

New versions download quietly in the background. When one is ready, you get a
prompt to restart. Restart, and you are on the latest version.

## On Mac

Because the app is not code-signed yet, macOS does not allow silent updates.
When a new version is available, the app shows a prompt with a **Download**
button. Click it to get the new `.dmg`, then drag the new app into
Applications, replacing the old one.

## Your notes are safe

Updates only replace the app itself. Your files stay where they are on disk,
and your workspaces stay connected. This guide refreshes to match the new
version, so it always describes what you actually have.
